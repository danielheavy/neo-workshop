Registration and Fee

The registration for the attendance at the NEO 2025 is per person. Attendance at the conference (in-person or virtually) that entitles to present up to two works (oral or poster) related to abstracts that were accepted by the NEO 2025. In-person attendance includes participation at all NEO events (tutorials, keynotes, and contributed talks), a welcome bag, and the gala dinner.
