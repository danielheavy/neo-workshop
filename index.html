12th International Workshop on Numerical and Evolutionary Optimization

Welcome to the web page of the NEO 2025

NEO invites researchers to present their work, encouraging original contributions but also accepting presentations of previously published works that fit to the scope of the event.
NEO will accept short abstracts that will appear in the book of abstracts of the event. Further, there is the opportunity to submit extended and other work related to the NEO to the special issue that will be published in the journal Mathematical and Computational Application. The NEO 2025 will support both in-person and virtual attendance.
